Our Story

Where Every Meal
Tells a Story

Nestled in Kabete Gardens along Lower Kabete Road, LA-PAZ is Nairobi's go-to destination for authentic Kenyan grills, refreshing local beverages, and a dining atmosphere that feels like home — only better.

From sizzling Nyama Choma paired with hand-rolled ugali and fresh kachumbari, to crispy Chicken Wings and stone-baked pizzas, every plate is crafted with pride and cooked to order.

  • Outdoor seating under the open sky
  • Private dining room for events
  • Big screen sport viewing
  • On-site car wash while you dine
  • Family & group-friendly
